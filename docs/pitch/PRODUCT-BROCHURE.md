## What your school gets

### Live tracking every parent can see

Every bus streams its position to the parents whose children are on board.
Turn-by-turn ETA to the next stop. No more "is she on the bus?" phone calls.

### RFID boarding — instant confirmation

The moment a child taps their RFID card at the bus door, the parent's phone
chimes: **"Wanjiku boarded Route 3 at 06:47."** Same when they alight at
school and again at home.

### One-tap SOS

Drivers hit one button. Ops, the head teacher, and every parent on that trip
know within seconds — with an exact GPS position and a live map link.

### Fees via M-Pesa in three taps

Parents receive an invoice, tap **Pay**, complete STK Push, and get an
**eTIMS-compliant receipt** the same second. Your bursar sees the payment
reconcile automatically.

### Fleet compliance made simple

Every vehicle carries its own compliance file — insurance, NTSA inspection,
PSV licence, driver certification. The system reminds you 30 days before
anything expires. No more surprise fines.

### Data protection, done right

Consent registry, parent-visible privacy dashboard, data-subject request
workflow. Fully compliant with Kenya's **Data Protection Act 2019** and the
**Children Act 2022**. You get the DPA registration paperwork templated.

### One login, every role

Head teacher, transport manager, bursar, driver, caretaker, parent — one
platform, each sees only what they need.

## Safety & trust — the promise

- **Every position is signed.** GPS pings and RFID scans use HMAC-SHA256 — nobody can spoof a "child boarded" message.
- **Every action is audited.** Every button press, every SMS, every payment — timestamped, immutable, court-defensible for 5 years.
- **Every parent controls their data.** One tap to withdraw consent, request an export, or request deletion.
- **Every session is protected.** Bank-grade encryption. FIDO2 support for staff. Session timeout on inactivity.

## Common questions

**Do we need to buy hardware?**
Only if you want RFID boarding scans and standalone GPS. We supply certified units at cost + 15%. Alternatively, driver phones do the tracking on day one.

**What if a parent has no smartphone?**
SMS-first: they still receive boarding, SOS, and payment alerts by SMS. A web link on the SMS opens the same live map on any browser.

**What if my internet is unreliable?**
The driver and caretaker apps work offline. Boarding events, SOS triggers, and payment initiations queue locally and sync automatically when connectivity returns.

**Can we keep our existing school ERP?**
Yes. Safari Shule is transport-first. We integrate with Zeraki, EduCloud, and any Excel-based system for student roster + fees.

**Where is the data stored?**
On AWS `af-south-1` (Cape Town) by default, or `eu-west-1` (Ireland). Cross-border transfer safeguards documented per DPA 2019.

**How much does it cost?**
See [PRICING.md](PRICING.md). Starter tier is KES 4,900/month; annual billing gets you two months free.
