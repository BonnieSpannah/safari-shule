## Transaction fees

We charge **0.5%** on successful M-Pesa fee collections, capped at KES 100 per transaction. Safaricom's own transaction charges pass through to the school unchanged.

**Example**: a parent pays KES 15,000 → Safari Shule fee = KES 75 (school pays this, not the parent).

For a school collecting KES 4M/month in fees, that's KES 20,000/month — typically 3–5× less than the labour saved on manual reconciliation.

## Frequently asked pricing questions

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No setup fee on Starter or Growth. Scale includes 2 weeks of white-glove onboarding. Enterprise is scoped separately.

**Q: What happens if we exceed our plan limits?**
A: We notify you when you hit 80% and 100%. Nothing is blocked — you're auto-billed the add-on rate for the overage that month, and we recommend upgrading if it's a permanent change.

**Q: Are updates and security patches included?**
A: Yes, always. Every plan gets the same product; tiers only differ in limits, support, and included volumes.

**Q: Can we downgrade?**
A: Yes, any time. Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle. Your data is preserved.

**Q: Do you offer a money-back guarantee?**
A: Yes — 60-day full refund if you're not satisfied. No questions, no clawbacks.

**Q: What about VAT?**
A: Where applicable (VAT-registered schools), VAT is invoiced separately. School tuition is generally VAT-exempt but transport services may not be — we invoice per Kenya Finance Act rules.

**Q: Can we pay in USD?**
A: Yes for Enterprise plans. Other plans are KES only.

**Q: What's your cancellation policy?**
A: Monthly plans: cancel any time; billed to end of current month. Annual plans: pro-rated refund of unused months (any full month refunded).
