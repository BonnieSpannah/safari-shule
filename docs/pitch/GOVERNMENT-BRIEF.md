# Safari Shule — Government & Institutional Partnership Brief

*For the Ministry of Education, National Transport & Safety Authority, county education offices, and civil-society partners in child-transport safety.*

---

## The opportunity

Kenya has approximately **40,000 school-owned or contracted vehicles** moving **1.4 million children** daily. There is currently **no standardized data pipeline** connecting this fleet to national safety, taxation, or education-management systems.

Safari Shule proposes a **public-private partnership** to deliver:

1. **NEMIS-aligned student-transport records** — every child's daily boarding + alighting event, tied to their UPI, in real time.
2. **NTSA vehicle-fleet transparency** — every school vehicle's registration, inspection, insurance, and PSV licence status, live.
3. **KRA eTIMS integration** — every school transport receipt hits KRA the same second.
4. **ODPC-compliant data handling** — DPA 2019 controls baked in from day one, evidence-ready for regulator audits.
5. **County-level fleet dashboards** — every county education officer sees the compliance status of every school in their jurisdiction.

We propose these are delivered via a **standard API** that any authorized government system can consume, with strict tenant-isolation guarantees, and independent audit.

## Proposed partnerships

### With the Ministry of Education

**Ask:** MoU authorizing NEMIS API integration + endorsement to schools.

**Value to MoE:**
- Real-time attendance data pipeline (opt-in per school).
- Verifiable transport-safety indicator per school for MoE inspection.
- CBC-aligned data on how students commute, informing infrastructure investment.
- A neutral, standardized platform that reduces per-school procurement risk.

**Safari Shule commitment:**
- Zero fee on the NEMIS data-sync API.
- Public dashboard of aggregate compliance metrics.
- Quarterly reports to MoE on adoption + coverage.
- Quarterly Kenyan-hosted training program for school administrators.

### With NTSA (National Transport & Safety Authority)

**Ask:** Recognized data source for school-transport fleet compliance + safety events.

**Value to NTSA:**
- Live inventory of school-fleet vehicles with inspection + insurance + PSV licence status.
- Real-time SOS + incident feed (anonymized where required).
- Post-incident audit trail — court-defensible chain of custody.
- Alert pipeline: 30 days before licence / inspection expiry, NTSA can proactively engage schools.

**Safari Shule commitment:**
- NTSA-format data exports (whatever schema NTSA specifies).
- Immutable audit log on every safety-relevant event, retained 5 years, cold-archived 12 years.
- Free tier for NTSA-owned dashboards.
- Threat-model reviews with NTSA cyber team.

### With county governments

**Ask:** Pilot in one county, endorse across public schools once proven.

**Value to counties:**
- County-level dashboard: which schools in my county are transport-compliant?
- Aggregate parent-alert channel for county-wide events (school closures, curfews).
- Fee-collection reconciliation for county-owned bursaries or transport subsidies.
- Data-backed cases for infrastructure investment (which routes need repair? which stops are unsafe?).

**Safari Shule commitment:**
- 50% off Growth-tier for public schools in the participating county.
- Free tier permanent for schools with fewer than 30 transported students.
- Dedicated county liaison.
- Quarterly county-level review meetings.

### With KRA (Kenya Revenue Authority)

**Ask:** Registered eTIMS partner status.

**Value to KRA:**
- Automated eTIMS transmission on every school-transport receipt.
- Reduced under-reporting via digital-first invoice generation.
- Aggregate reports on school-transport revenue for policy design.

**Safari Shule commitment:**
- Every fee receipt transmitted to eTIMS the same second.
- Void + credit-note flows fully instrumented.
- Zero-touch KRA PIN validation for tenants.

### With civil society (child-safety NGOs, parent associations)

**Ask:** Endorsement + parent-awareness campaign co-branding.

**Value:**
- Parent-facing safety education materials, co-branded, distributed via the parent app.
- Anonymized safety-incident data for advocacy.
- Free tier for schools serving vulnerable populations.

## Data governance guarantees

- **Sovereign hosting** — all customer data hosted in AWS `af-south-1` (Cape Town) or on-premise if required by a specific tenant.
- **No third-party data sharing** without explicit consent from each affected data subject.
- **Government access** — only via lawful process (court order or written statutory request); every access logged and reported to the affected tenant.
- **Independent audit** — annual SOC 2 Type II; annual ODPC-alignment review; annual DPIA per module.
- **Public transparency report** — published annually, listing every government-access request received (aggregate + count).
- **Data minimization** — we collect only what's needed to run school transport safely; no marketing, no location-tracking outside of active trips.

## Pilot proposal

**Phase 1 — 90 days · 1 county · 10 pilot schools · zero cost**

- Safari Shule deployed at cost.
- One county-education-office liaison assigned.
- Weekly progress reviews.
- Success metrics:
  - 100% of pilot schools issuing eTIMS receipts by day 30.
  - 90% of transported students onboarded with parent app by day 60.
  - Zero DPA / NTSA / KRA compliance findings by day 90.
  - Baseline SOS response time measurement.

**Phase 2 — 12 months · county-wide public + private schools**

- Standard pricing with public-school discount.
- Quarterly county-level dashboards published.
- Joint annual review with participating agencies.

**Phase 3 — national rollout**

- Formal MoU with MoE / NTSA / KRA / ODPC.
- Standardized data-sharing APIs.
- Regional expansion (Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda) with same partnership shape.
